| **"End and carry moves forward"** | "End and save", other variants |
| **"Use bonus moves"** | "Use banked moves" |

### Planned rename: "bonus round" → "victory round"

The phase where a player has reached the target score with regular moves still
remaining is currently called the **bonus round** in code and UI. This will be
renamed to **victory round** in a future session.

**Why:** "Bonus round" creates confusion with "bonus moves" — players can't easily
tell the two concepts apart. "Victory round" captures the right idea: you've already
won, this is your lap of honour to keep scoring. "Bonus moves" captures the right
idea for the moves: extra moves earned through scoring, available any time regular
moves hit zero.

**Scope of the rename:**
- UI strings: button labels, prompt text, transition screen copy
- Variable names: `bonusRoundActive`, `setBonusRoundActive`, `startBonusRound`,
  `showBonusPrompt`, `bonusRoundScore`, `preBonusScore`
- Comments throughout the campaign file
- `docs/DESIGN.md` — Bonus Round section heading and body

Do not rename until a dedicated session — it touches many places and should be done
carefully in one pass.
