## What was done this session (2026-03-22, v9 session)

### Campaign v1.13 — level intro screens

A `LevelIntroScreen` component and `LEVEL_INTROS` data array (8 entries, one per level)
were added. The router gained a `'levelIntro'` screen state. Both `handleSelectLevel`
and `handleNextLevel` now route through the intro before starting play.

Content per level:
- L1: Match 3+, swap mechanics, reach target score
- L2: Special tiles (line/bomb/cross/supernova/hypernova), combining specials
- L3: Time attack — 60s, no move limit, +5s triggers (special, combo ×5+, 5k pts)
- L4: Combos & cascades — score multipliers, special combinations
- L5: Bonus moves — earn 1 per 10k pts, carry between levels, use or save
- L6: Time attack round 2 — 2 min, prompted bonus move usage on expiry
- L7: Almost there — stars needed to unlock L8
- L8: Final level

### Campaign v1.14 — time attack star rating bug fix

Stars were evaluated the moment the timer hit 0 (or the player dismissed the time-up
prompt), before pending tile falls and cascade scoring finished.

Fix: replaced direct `setGameState` calls in the timer expiry path and `endLevelSaveMoves`
with a `pendingTimeExpiry` state flag. A new useEffect waits for `isAnimating === false`
and `combo === 0` before evaluating the final score and calling `setGameState`.
`pendingTimeExpiry` is also cleared in the retry reset block.

### Campaign v1.15 — transition screen fields + campaign complete screen

**Transition screen** now shows:
- Moves used (non-time-attack levels only, shown as "N of M")
- Best combo (if >0)
- Specials created (if >0, tracked via new `specialsCreatedRef`)
- Bonus moves earned this level (if >0)
- Bonus moves in pool total (if >0)
- Campaign running total score (purple box, sum of best scores across all levels)
- "See Results" label on last level instead of "Next Level →"

"Banked" → "bonus" throughout the transition screen.

**Campaign complete screen** (shown after L8, replaces old map redirect):
- Total campaign score, stars/40, per-level score + star breakdown
- "Play Again" → level select, records kept
- "Reset all and start again" → clears all `CAMPAIGN_KEYS` localStorage, resets
  `levelStars`/`levelHighScores` state, routes directly to L1 intro

**Specials tracking:** `specialsCreatedRef` incremented at both special-placement
sites — `removeMatches` (line ~1784) and `activateSpecialCombination` (line ~1684).
Reset in retry block.

**Data flow:** `handleLevelEnd` now passes `movesUsed`, `bestCombo`, `specialsCreated`,
`bonusMovePoolTotal` to `onLevelComplete`. `handleLevelComplete` in `Match3Campaign`
computes `campaignTotalScore` from `newHighScores` and adds it to `transitionData`.

### Campaign v1.16 — game over screen improvements

The game over overlay now shows:
- Score formatted with `toLocaleString`
- Personal best score + star rating in a dark inset box ("No previous best" on first attempt)
- Primary buttons: Retry + View Map (unchanged layout)
- Subtle "Start over from Level 1 (keeps records)" link below primary buttons

`CampaignGame` receives three new props from `Match3Campaign`:
- `highScore` — `levelHighScores[activeLevelIndex] || 0`
- `bestStars` — `levelStars[activeLevelIndex] || 0`
- `onStartOver` — navigates to L1 intro without clearing records
